rec30s

A simple, 30 second long audio recorder.
Stop or Pause when you need to.
At a press of a button.


Instructions

  1. Press the button to record.
  2. End recording anytime,
    or countiue until 30 seconds.
  3. Listen to what you made.
  4. Pause playback whenever.
  5. Repeat.
